Features

Everything You Need to Go Paperless

VizoScan provides a clean, fast, and reliable scanning experience on any Android device.

📷
Document Scanning

Capture any document with your camera. Automatic edge detection delivers sharp, clean scans every time.

🪪
ID Card Scanning

Scan both front and back of ID cards in a single document. Perfect for KYC and verification workflows.

📑
PDF Generation

Combine multiple scanned pages into a single, print-ready PDF document instantly on device.

🚀
One-Tap Sharing

Share your PDF via WhatsApp, email, Drive, or any app — directly from the document viewer.

🌙
Dark & Light Mode

Fully supports Android's system theme. Switch between dark and light mode without missing a beat.

✨
Material Design 3

Built with Flutter and Material You design language for a modern, accessible, and beautiful interface.

How It Works

Three Steps to a Perfect PDF

Getting your documents digitized has never been easier.

1
Open & Scan

Tap the scan button, point your camera at the document, and capture. Auto-detection handles the rest.

2
Review Pages

Browse all scanned pages with the swipe viewer. Add more pages or delete any you don't need.

3
Export & Share

Tap Share to export a PDF and send it anywhere — email, cloud storage, messaging apps, and more.
